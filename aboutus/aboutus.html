About Us


The fundraising committee is pleased because the hall has proved to be extremely popular and therefore lucrative. However, it is becoming increasingly difficult to manage the number of bookings that are coming in and to ensure that payments have been made. Although the hall is well used, there are times when it is not in use. The committee would like to have a better understanding of what the hall is being used for, how much revenue is being generated and who is using it. At the last fundraising committee meeting, members voted to allocate some funding to commission a computer application to manage the business created by the sports hall. The committee members are extremely busy people and it was decided that it would be convenient to be able to access the application over the Internet.
