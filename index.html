Welcome to the Webberbridge Sports Hall Website


Introduction

The Webberbridge Sports Hall is a great facility for the local residents of the Webberbridge Village and local area.


Membership can be obtained after you or your child has attended their first Induction Session (free). This is so you or your child is familiarised with the facilities on site and is familiar with health and safety . To book onto one of our Induction sessions, please visit the Induction page and fill out the form.
