Bog Snorkelling


Ever wanted to be the fastest at bog snorkelling? Well now you can with our new exciting Bog Snorkeller's course!


Bog Snorkelling is a Welsh idea conceived in the Neuadd Arms pub in Llawrtyd Wells whereby competitiors snorkel in a 60 foot cold and murky mud bog in order to get the record time without using traditional swimming strokes.


Our Bog measures 80 feet which is the perfect size to challenge our would be bog champions! Remember to bring your own wetsuit, flippers, snorkel mask, cloth and towels and we'll provide the buckets of clean water!
