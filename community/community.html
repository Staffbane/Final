Community


The village of Webberbridge has an active fundraising committee, Mrs Weatherington is the chair of the committee and she is proud of the contribution everyone has made raising money through all sorts of events over the years including tombolas, welly wanging competitions, tugs of war. Recently it raised money to convert an old barn gifted to the village by a retired farmer David Plhough-Farrows into a multipurpose sports hall. David lived in Webberbridge al his life and played cricket, football and dart for the village team and was the inaugural winner of the Webberbridge Bog-Snorkelling challenge which is run according to the Irish Bog-Snorkelling Association Rules.
