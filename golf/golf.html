Golf


Golf is an outdoor sport involving a series of holes, the objective of the game is to hit the ball with as few strokes as possible and land the ball in the hole at the end of each hole. Lowest score wins.


Learn eqtiquette sportmanship as you shake hands with other respectable players, experience different formats of this game such as texas-scramble, handicaps will enable players of all abilities to enjoy this activity and our on course coaches will ensure you can get each hole under par!


Our course features 18 breathtaking courses with beautfully tended gardens, trees, bunkers, ditches, ponds and mounds to keep things exciting for golfers of all ages. Please ensure you wear appropriate clothing: Slacks, long shorts, skorts or ankle length pants, collared golf or sports shirts and of course don't forget your golf shoes and golf clubs. Caddies and Golf balls will be provided as well as refreshments.
